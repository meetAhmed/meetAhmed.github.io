Subscription Support

Managing Your Subscription:

All subscriptions are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. To manage your subscription:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select PortionPal
  5. Manage or cancel your subscription

For billing issues: Contact Apple Support directly
