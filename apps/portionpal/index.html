Everything You Need in One App

Comprehensive health tracking designed for simplicity and effectiveness

Complete Nutrition Tracking

Track calories, carbs, fats, and protein for every meal. Search our extensive food database, scan barcodes, or enter items manually. Save your favorite foods for lightning-fast logging.

Blood Glucose Monitoring

Log readings from any glucometer with helpful context tags like "Before Meal" or "After Meal". Supports both mg/dL and mmol/L units. Watch patterns emerge over time.

Hydration Tracking

Log your water intake throughout the day and stay on top of your hydration goals. Track every glass and see your daily progress at a glance.

Automatic Activity Tracking

Seamlessly syncs with Apple Health to fetch your daily step count and active energy burned. No manual entry needed - just move and watch your progress build.

Weight Trends & Progress

Record your weight daily or weekly and visualize your progress over time. See the bigger picture with clear, motivating charts that show you're moving in the right direction.

Daily Dashboard

See everything that matters in one clean timeline: glucose readings, meals with macros, water intake, steps, active energy, and weight trends. All in one place.

Home Screen Widget

Check your daily calorie and macro progress (carbs, fats, protein) right from your home screen. No need to open the app - see your intake at a glance.

Apple Health Integration

Connects seamlessly with Apple Health for automatic step tracking, active energy monitoring, and comprehensive health data syncing.
