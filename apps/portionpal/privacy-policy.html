Information We Collect

Personal Health Data (Stored Locally Only)

To calculate your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) and daily calorie goal, we may collect:

  • Age
  • Height
  • Weight
  • Gender
  • Activity level

Important: All of this data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this information. Providing this information during onboarding is optional - you can skip these steps and manually set your daily calorie goal.

Health Tracking Data (Stored Locally Only)

When you use the app, the following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Blood glucose readings
  • Food intake and calorie logs
  • Steps and active energy data (synced from Apple Health with your permission)
  • Weight measurements
  • Water intake
  • Meal logs and timestamps

We do not have access to any of this personal health data.

Analytics Data (Non-Personal)

We use Firebase Analytics to collect:

  • App crash data
  • App usage statistics
  • Feature usage patterns

Important: This analytics data is:

  • Anonymous - not linked to any specific user
  • Not tracked by user identity
  • Used solely to improve app performance and user experience
  • Does not include any of your personal health information

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name or personal identification
  • Your email address or phone number
  • User accounts or login credentials (the app requires no sign-up)
  • Your personal health data on our servers
  • Any data that identifies you personally

How We Use Your Information

Local Data Usage

Your personal health data stored on your device is used to:

  • Calculate personalized calorie and macro goals
  • Track your health metrics over time
  • Provide insights and progress charts
  • Sync across your Apple devices via iCloud (if enabled)

Analytics Usage

Anonymous analytics data is used to:

  • Identify and fix app crashes
  • Understand which features are most useful
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Guide future app development

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All personal health data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is secured using iOS security features
  • Only you have access to your health data

iCloud Backup

  • Your health data is automatically backed up to your personal iCloud account
  • iCloud backup is encrypted and controlled by Apple
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data
  • You can access your data even after reinstalling the app or switching devices
  • You can delete your iCloud data through iOS Settings → iCloud → Manage Storage

Firebase Remote Config

  • We use Firebase Remote Config to deliver app settings and configurations
  • No personal or health data is written to Firebase
  • This service only delivers app settings, not user data

Third-Party Services

Apple Health

  • The app integrates with Apple Health to sync step count and active energy data
  • This integration requires your explicit permission
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings
  • We do not store Health data on any external servers

Apple In-App Purchase

  • All subscriptions are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • Apple handles all payment information
  • We do not have access to your payment details
  • For billing issues, contact Apple Support directly

Firebase (Google)

  • Used only for anonymous analytics and remote configuration
  • Does not receive any personal health data
  • Subject to Google's privacy policy for Firebase services

Your Data Rights

Data Access

  • You have full access to all your data stored locally in the app
  • You can view, edit, or delete any health entry at any time

Data Deletion

  • Uninstall the app: All locally stored data will be deleted
  • iCloud data: You can delete your iCloud backup through iOS Settings → iCloud → Manage Storage
  • Analytics data: Anonymous analytics cannot be deleted as it's not linked to you

Data Portability

  • Your data is stored in standard formats on your device
  • Data syncs across your Apple devices via iCloud

Children's Privacy

PortionPal is not intended for use by children under the age of 15. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 15. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Medical Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: PortionPal is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

  • This app does not provide medical advice
  • Do not rely solely on this app for health decisions
  • Always consult with qualified healthcare providers for medical advice
  • The app may contain errors or inaccuracies
  • Consult your doctor or healthcare provider regularly
  • Show this app to your healthcare provider to verify if it's appropriate for your health needs
  • In critical situations, always seek immediate medical attention

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting Firebase Analytics (the only service where any data is sent), we will:

  • Notify affected users via app notification or email (if available)
  • Disclose the nature of the breach
  • Provide guidance on protective measures

Note: Since personal health data is stored only on your device, it cannot be breached through our systems.

International Users

Our app is available internationally. By using PortionPal:

  • You consent to your analytics data being processed in accordance with this policy
  • Your personal health data remains on your device and in your iCloud account
  • We comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR where applicable

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
